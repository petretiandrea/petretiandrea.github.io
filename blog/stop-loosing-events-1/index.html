Stop Losing Events How the Outbox Pattern Guarantees Consistency in Distributed Systems

In modern microservices, ensuring consistency between database updates and emitted events is notoriously tricky. This article explores the challenges of reliably sending events without losing or duplicating them. We start with a naive approach, then dive into the Transactional Outbox Pattern, exploring both single-instance and distributed scenarios. You'll learn how to design robust, scalable event pipelines that maintain consistency across services, even in the face of crashes, retries, or multiple relay replicas.
